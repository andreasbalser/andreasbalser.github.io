Usability-Test

Analytical and empirical Usability-Test of the BürgerBot Würzburg

Project University-Projekt in Group of 5
Task Conduct an analytic and an empiric usability-test, comparing two different Applications with regard to their usability
Duration 6 Months
Experiences Cognitive Walkthrough, creating Personas, analytic & empiric Usability-Tests, scientific work

The "BürgerBot Würzburg" is an online tool for civic engagement, which enables users to participate in local affairs via a modern Chatbot technology. For our project, we compared the "BürgerBot" with a traditional online form of the city of Würzburg with regard to their usability. We evaluated both products analytically with a Cognitive Walkthrough and then conducted an empiric study with a total of 25 participants.
